Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I play Rotate & Shadow?

A: The goal is simple: rotate the abstract 3D object on your screen until its shadow aligns perfectly with the target silhouette in the background. Once you reach 92% or higher matching, the level is cleared!

Q: Does the game require an internet connection?

A: No. Rotate & Shadow is designed for offline play, making it a perfect companion for travel or places with limited connectivity.

Q: The game is lagging or crashing. What should I do?

A: Please ensure your device is running the latest OS version. Try closing background apps or restarting your device. If problems persist, contact us with your device model and OS version.

Q: Will more levels be added?

A: Yes! We are constantly working on new geometric challenges and creative shapes. Keep your app updated for the latest content.
